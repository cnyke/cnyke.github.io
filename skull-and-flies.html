Skull and Flies

Mixed Media Digital Illustration

A detailed exploration of mortality and transformation, this piece combines traditional illustration techniques with digital enhancement. The intricate details of the skull juxtaposed with the dynamic movement of the flies creates a compelling narrative about life, death, and the cycles of nature.

Technical Details

This piece was created using a combination of traditional and digital techniques. The initial sketch was done in graphite, followed by digital enhancement and texture work in Adobe Photoshop. The flies were individually illustrated to create a sense of movement and depth in the composition.
