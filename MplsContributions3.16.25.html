Minneapolis Campaign Contributions

This visualization shows campaign contributions for Minneapolis political races. Each bubble represents a campaign, with the size of the bubble proportional to the total amount of contributions received. Bubbles are color-coded and grouped by ward or district. Hover over any bubble to see detailed information about the candidate and their total contributions. Use the controls below to explore different years and offices.
Data source: City of Minneapolis. (2025). Candidate & Economic Interest Filing. [dataset]. MapLight. Retrieved 3/16/2025 from https://minneapolis.maplight.com/public/transaction-search.
